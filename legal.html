Terms, Submissions & Creator Rights

1. Creator Rights & Ownership

Art Allergy operates on a creator-first philosophy. Independent authors, poets, and visual artists retain 100% of the underlying copyrights to their work. We do not demand long-term exclusive distribution grids or ownership of intellectual property.

When we contract a publication, we acquire only a limited license to print, distribute, and digitally display the specific agreed-upon run of the work. All other rights remain strictly with the creator.

2. Copyright Protection

All visual layouts, books, zines, and poetry published by Art Allergy are copyright protected by their respective authors. The "Art Allergy" logo, interactive designs, and website code are copyrighted by Art Allergy. Unauthorized commercial reproduction, cloning, or distribution of our featured catalog works will be met with swift legal objection.

3. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Art Allergy does not deploy tracking cookies, third-party analytics scripts, or data-mining algorithms. We collect zero visitor data. Personal information and manuscripts submitted through the Pitch Terminal are held in strict confidence and are never shared or sold.

4. Submission Policy

Manuscript drafts and pitches submitted via our Pitch Terminal are reviewed confidentially by our New York and London editorial desks. We do not claim any right or ownership over submitted materials. If a draft is not selected for publication, it is permanently deleted from our records.
