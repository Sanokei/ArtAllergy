Our Philosophy

The Manifesto

We have a profound allergy to the sanitized, the corporate, and the algorithmically optimized. Modern publishing has become an industry of risk aversion, transforming raw human expression into predictable commodities. We reject the focus groups, the content filters, and the digital gates that keep underground voices on the margins.

Art Allergy was born in the drafty lofts of Brooklyn and the damp brick arches of East London. We publish visual literature, experimental prose, street photography, and DIY zines. Our editions are printed in small, numbered runs—tangible, permanent, and unapologetically flawed.

Creator Registry

Send Us Your Work

We are open to submissions from creators based in the New York or London metropolitan areas. We publish zines, visual art prints, poetry, essays, and experimental novellas.

• No academic background or publication history required.

• Send 5–10 pages of prose/poetry, or a concept pitch.

• Creators retain full ownership of their underlying copyrights.
